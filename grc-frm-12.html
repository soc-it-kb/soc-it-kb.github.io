Welcome to the Application Onboarding Pack

This form ensures every new application is properly supported, secured, and documented before going live. It takes 30–60 minutes to complete. Each section clearly states who fills it in.

1
Business Owner startsSections 1 & 2 — identity, purpose, users
2
IT/Security take overSections 3, 4 & 5 — technical & security
3
Complete togetherSection 6 — support model & change
4
All approveSections 7 & 8 — RACI, SLAs & sign-off

🟦 Business / App Owner

You know the application best — its purpose, users, and business context.

  • Sections 1 & 2 (mandatory)
  • Parts of Section 6
  • Final sign-off in Section 8

🟩 IT & Security Team

Technical configuration, security controls, and infrastructure details.

  • Sections 3, 4 & 5 (mandatory)
  • CMDB entry and service catalogue publication
  • Security sign-off in Section 8

🟨 Completed Together

Sections where both parties need to agree on shared responsibilities.

  • Section 6 — support model and risks
  • Section 7 — RACI and SLAs
  • Section 8 — acceptance checklist
📖
Glossary of Key Terms
Quick reference — expand as needed
Business Owner
Senior stakeholder accountable for the application and its business outcomes
Application Owner
Day-to-day manager of the application from the business side
Service Owner
IT accountability for overall service delivery and support model
Technical Owner
Engineer responsible for infrastructure, hosting, and technical operation
Security Owner
Security specialist accountable for the application's security posture
CMDB
Configuration Management Database — the IT record of all assets and services
OLA
Operational Level Agreement — internal IT service commitments
RPO / RTO
Recovery Point / Time Objective — how much data loss and downtime are acceptable
RACI
Responsible · Accountable · Consulted · Informed — every activity has exactly one Accountable owner
CAB
Change Advisory Board — approves significant or high-risk changes
