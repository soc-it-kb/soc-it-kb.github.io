EM-RB-06-01: Initial Triage & Validation

Confirm incident validity, preserve evidence, and determine initial scope for a threatening or harassing email campaign. First runbook in the EM-PB-06 four-part series.

Section 1
Objective

Confirm incident validity, preserve evidence, and determine the initial scope of the threatening or harassing email campaign.

Section 2
Trigger & Invocation
🔗 Invoked from EM-PB-06 👥 Executed by SOC Tier-1
Section 3
Main Run — Triage Procedure
1
Validate Email
1
Confirm the Message ID
2
Confirm the sender address and exact delivery time
3
Confirm all recipients or the distribution list targeted
2
Preserve Evidence
1
Export full email headers
2
Capture the full email body unaltered — do not edit or paraphrase
3
Secure any attachments and URLs without executing them
Do not click links or open attachments — preserve in sandbox only
3
Identify Scope
1
Count the total number of recipients
2
Identify affected departments, geographic regions, and any VIP or privileged recipients
4
Initial Classification
1
Classify the email content into one of the following:
  • Harassment only — no direct threat
  • Threat or intimidation present
Section 4
Decision Points

After initial classification, apply the following decision logic:

✅ No threat indicators

Exit to standard abuse or harassment workflow — do not escalate within this series

🔴 Threat indicators present

Escalate to Tier-2 and proceed to EM-RB-06-02 (Threat & Credibility Analysis)

Section 5
Outputs

✅ Incident ticket created with all evidence attached
✅ Evidence preserved and secured
✅ Tier-2 escalation initiated if threat indicators confirmed

Incident ticket
Email headers export
Email body capture
Recipient scope list
