Standard Operating Procedures

Standard Operating Procedures across SOC, IT Operations & Attack Surface Management, and Governance, Risk & Compliance. SOPs sit above Playbooks and Runbooks in the documentation hierarchy — they define the operating standards; Playbooks and Runbooks execute them. Every domain SOP defines who does what, in what order, under what authority, and against which compliance framework.

SOPs establish mandatory operating standards across the institution and its agencies — who does what, in what order, under what authority, and against which framework. They govern the execution of Playbooks and Runbooks, define escalation, communication, and compliance obligations, and provide the audit trail that demonstrates due diligence to internal and external scrutiny.
