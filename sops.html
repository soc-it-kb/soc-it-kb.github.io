Standard Operating Procedures

Authoritative SOPs governing how the Security Operations Centre detects, triages, escalates, responds to, and recovers from cybersecurity incidents. SOPs define mandatory processes, roles, decision points, and control requirements.

Governance layer All tiers Mandatory
SOPs are mandatory. Deviations require a documented exception. SOPs are reviewed annually or following major incidents.
Scope

These SOPs apply to all teams involved in security operations and incident response:

  • Global SOC
  • Regional SOCs
  • MDR partner (where contractually applicable)
  • Local IT / Cyber teams engaged in incident response

Governance notes
  • SOPs are mandatory for all in-scope teams — compliance is not optional
  • Any deviation from an SOP must be documented with a formal exception request
  • SOPs are reviewed annually as a minimum, or immediately following a major incident
  • Updates to SOPs must go through the change control process defined in Governance & Change Log
