About work instructions

Work instructions are the most granular level of operational documentation in this knowledge base. They are designed to be:

  • Tool-agnostic where possible — steps describe the action and expected outcome, not just menu paths that change with software updates
  • SOAR-ready — structured to be translated directly into automated playbook actions
  • Linked to runbooks and playbooks — each WI is called from a specific step in a parent Runbook or Playbook
  • Evidence-focused — each step notes what should be recorded for the incident record
