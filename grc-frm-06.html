GRC-FRM-06: SaaS / Cloud Supplier Security Assessment Questionnaire

For suppliers providing SaaS, hosted, managed, or cloud services that process organisational data or provide critical business functionality.

Supplier Profile and Service Scope

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
SP-01 Describe the service being provided, including the business purpose, delivery model, and whether the service is on-premises, hosted, managed service, or SaaS.
SP-02 State whether you will act as controller, processor, sub-processor, or another role in relation to our data.
SP-03 List the categories of data you will process for us, including personal data, special category data, confidential information, credentials, logs, and metadata.
SP-04 Identify the countries in which our data will be stored, processed, backed up, or remotely accessed.
SP-05 Describe any integrations with our systems, APIs, identity services, or third-party platforms.
SP-06 State whether any of our data will be used for analytics, service improvement, model training, or product development.

Governance, Risk and Assurance

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
GV-01 Is there a named individual accountable for information security for the service? Please provide role title and reporting line.
GV-02 Do you maintain formal information security policies and standards, and how often are they reviewed and approved?
GV-03 Do you maintain a formal risk assessment or risk register for the service and supporting environment?
GV-04 How are security exceptions or control deviations approved, recorded, and tracked to closure?
GV-05 Please list current certifications or independent assurance reports relevant to the service, including scope and expiry date.
GV-06 Have you experienced any material security, privacy, or resilience incidents in the last 24 months that are relevant to the service being offered?

Personnel Security and Awareness

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
PS-01 Are background screening checks performed for personnel with access to customer data or production systems, subject to legal and local requirements?
PS-02 Do employment contracts or equivalent terms include confidentiality and acceptable use obligations?
PS-03 Is mandatory security and privacy awareness training provided at induction and at least annually thereafter?
PS-04 Do staff in privileged, engineering, or support roles receive role-specific security training?
PS-05 Is there a defined joiner, mover, leaver process, including timely revocation of access on exit or role change?

Asset, Architecture and Change Management

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
AR-01 Do you maintain an inventory of systems, applications, cloud services, repositories, and key assets supporting the service?
AR-02 Please provide a high-level architecture summary showing major components, trust boundaries, hosting locations, and critical dependencies.
AR-03 Are development, test, staging, and production environments segregated?
AR-04 Are changes to production systems formally assessed, approved, tested, and documented before release?
AR-05 Do you maintain secure configuration baselines or hardened build standards for relevant platforms?

Identity and Access Management

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
IA-01 Is multi-factor authentication enforced for all privileged access, remote access, and administrative access to systems supporting the service?
IA-02 Do you support customer single sign-on using SAML or OIDC, and role-based access control for customer administrators and users?
IA-03 Are privileged accounts separate from standard user accounts?
IA-04 Are access rights approved on a least-privilege basis and reviewed periodically?
IA-05 Are service accounts, API keys, and machine credentials controlled, monitored, and rotated?
IA-06 Are break-glass or emergency access accounts controlled, logged, and periodically tested?

Data Protection and Privacy

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
DP-01 Do you maintain records of processing activities or equivalent documentation for the service?
DP-02 Do you support data subject rights requests, including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, and objection, where applicable?
DP-03 Please describe your data retention, archival, and secure deletion processes for customer data, backups, and logs.
DP-04 Do you transfer personal data outside the UK or EEA? If yes, please identify destinations and transfer mechanisms used.
DP-05 Have you completed privacy impact assessments, DPIAs, or equivalent privacy risk reviews where required for the service?
DP-06 How do you ensure data minimisation, purpose limitation, and privacy by design within the service?
DP-07 Is customer data used for any purpose beyond delivering the contracted service, and can such uses be disabled or contractually restricted?

Encryption, Key Management and Secrets

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
EK-01 Is customer data encrypted in transit using current TLS standards or equivalent secure protocols?
EK-02 Is customer data encrypted at rest for databases, file storage, backups, and logs where appropriate?
EK-03 Who manages encryption keys, and how are keys protected, rotated, and access-controlled?
EK-04 Are secrets, tokens, and credentials stored in a managed secrets solution rather than hard-coded or manually shared?
EK-05 Are certificates centrally managed and monitored for expiry or misconfiguration?

Logging, Monitoring and Detection

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
LM-01 Are authentication events, privileged actions, administrative changes, data exports, and security-relevant events logged?
LM-02 Are logs retained for a defined period, protected against tampering, and regularly reviewed?
LM-03 Do you operate centralised security monitoring, SIEM, or equivalent alerting for the service and supporting environment?
LM-04 Is security monitoring performed 24x7 for critical services, either internally or through a third party?
LM-05 Can customer administrators access audit logs relevant to their tenant or service instance?

Vulnerability, Patch and Configuration Management

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
VP-01 Do you perform routine internal and external vulnerability scanning for systems supporting the service?
VP-02 Please state vulnerability remediation SLAs for critical, high, medium, and low findings.
VP-03 Are operating systems, applications, cloud services, containers, and third-party libraries patched or remediated through a formal process?
VP-04 Are configuration compliance checks or baseline reviews performed to identify drift from secure configurations?
VP-05 Do you conduct independent penetration testing at least annually and after significant changes?

Secure Development and Application Security

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
SD-01 Do you maintain a documented secure development lifecycle applicable to the service?
SD-02 Are code changes peer-reviewed before release to production?
SD-03 Do you use application security testing such as SAST, DAST, software composition analysis, or secrets scanning?
SD-04 Are developers trained in secure coding relevant to the languages and technologies used?
SD-05 Is production data prevented from use in non-production environments, or masked where use is necessary?
SD-06 Do you maintain an inventory of software components, dependencies, or SBOM-equivalent records for the service?

SaaS Security, Hosting and Tenant Isolation

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
SS-01 Is the service single-tenant, multi-tenant, or available in both models?
SS-02 How is logical separation between customers enforced and tested in the service?
SS-03 Please describe the hosting model, including cloud provider or data centre arrangements and regional resilience design.
SS-04 What customer-facing security features are available, such as MFA, SSO, IP allow-listing, session controls, API security controls, or administrator role segregation?
SS-05 Can customer data be exported in a usable format on demand and at termination of service?

Incident Response and Breach Management

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
IR-01 Do you maintain a documented incident response plan covering cyber incidents, data breaches, and service-impacting events?
IR-02 How are incidents classified, escalated, and communicated internally and to customers?
IR-03 What are your breach notification timelines and minimum notification content for affected customers?
IR-04 Is the incident response process tested through tabletop, simulation, or live exercises at least annually?
IR-05 Are post-incident reviews performed and tracked through corrective actions to closure?

Business Continuity, Backup and Disaster Recovery

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
BC-01 Do you maintain documented business continuity and disaster recovery plans for the service?
BC-02 Please state recovery time objective and recovery point objective commitments relevant to the service.
BC-03 Are backups performed, protected from unauthorised alteration or deletion, and tested for recovery?
BC-04 When was the last disaster recovery or service restoration test, and what was the outcome?
BC-05 Please identify any significant single points of failure or material dependencies that could affect service resilience.

Physical and Environmental Security

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
PH-01 Are offices, data centres, and other facilities with relevant systems protected by appropriate physical access controls?
PH-02 Are visitor access, contractor access, and delivery access controlled and recorded where appropriate?
PH-03 Are environmental protections in place for facilities hosting critical systems, such as fire detection, suppression, power resilience, and temperature control?
PH-04 If colocation or cloud hosting is used, do you rely on third-party facility assurance and certification rather than directly managing physical controls?

Third Parties, Sub-processors and Supply Chain

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
TP-01 Please provide a current list of sub-processors and material subcontractors relevant to the service, including the service each provides and processing location.
TP-02 Do you assess third-party suppliers for security, privacy, and resilience risk before engagement and periodically thereafter?
TP-03 Are contractual security and privacy requirements imposed on sub-processors and material subcontractors?
TP-04 How much notice will customers receive before the appointment or replacement of a sub-processor that may access customer data?
TP-05 Do you maintain contingency plans for failure or compromise of critical third-party dependencies?

AI Use, Analytics and Emerging Technology

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
AI-01 Does the service include AI, machine learning, automated decision support, or similar emerging technology features?
AI-02 Is customer data used to train shared, internal, or customer-specific models, and can this be disabled?
AI-03 Have privacy, security, and model risk assessments been completed for relevant AI-enabled features?
AI-04 What controls are in place to prevent data leakage, unauthorised retention, or inappropriate outputs from AI-enabled features?

Contractual, Commercial and Exit Management

Ref Question Supplier Response Evidence Reference Reviewer Notes
CT-01 Do contractual terms include minimum security, confidentiality, breach notification, audit, and data protection obligations relevant to the service?
CT-02 Do you maintain cyber insurance or equivalent financial coverage relevant to the services provided?
CT-03 Are customers entitled to receive updated assurance information or complete periodic reassessments during the contract term?
CT-04 Please describe exit support, data return, data deletion, and transition assistance available at contract termination.
CT-05 Will you notify customers of material changes to ownership, hosting model, sub-processors, security posture, or regulatory status that may affect risk?

Supplier Declaration: The supplier confirms that the responses provided are accurate to the best of its knowledge and that any material change affecting security posture, data processing arrangements, sub-processors, hosting model, or relevant certifications will be notified without undue delay.
