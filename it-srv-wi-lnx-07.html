Protect data at rest and ensure recoverability on Linux servers.

Prerequisites
Backup solution available
Step-by-step instructions
1
Enable disk or volume encryption where supported
2
Identify sensitive data locations
3
Ensure backups are encrypted and protected
4
Perform and document restore tests
Validation & verification
Encryption enabled and confirmed
Restore tests successful and documented
Rollback & troubleshooting
Suspend encryption only for approved maintenance — re-enable immediately after
Common errors & tips
Untested backups are unreliable — always document restore test results with date and outcome
Backup encryption protects against data exposure even if backup media is compromised
