GRC-IRP-ANNEX-TEMPLATE: Local Incident Response Annex

Each operating unit completes this annex to localise GRC-IRP-01 with the contacts, regulators, language, and timing requirements specific to its jurisdiction. The completed annex is held by the local LCSO/CSLC and reviewed at least annually.

Local annex Adapts GRC-IRP-01 Annual review

Template

📌 How to use this template. Each operating unit (agency, country, subsidiary) maintains one completed annex. The annex extends — never overrides — the master plan in GRC-IRP-01.

Owner: Local CSLC (Cyber Security Local Coordinator) or LCSO (Local Cyber Security Officer). Review: Annually, plus after any material change to local contacts, regulators, or insurance. Storage: Locally controlled; copy registered with central GRC team.

Classification: Confidential — Level 3, consistent with GRC-IRP-01.
