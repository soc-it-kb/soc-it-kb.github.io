How to use this hub

📋 Policy management

Locate the latest approved policies under Governance. Always use the version published here — not local copies.

🏢 Supplier assurance

Use the Risk domain for supplier security assessments — questionnaires, scored assessment records, and reviewer work instructions.

⚠️ Risk & exceptions

Log and track risk acceptances, policy exceptions, and baseline exceptions via the Risk domain registers.

✅ Compliance monitoring

Review baseline compliance status, validation runs, and drift remediation via the Compliance domain registers.

🔍 Audit & assurance

Use registers and validation run records as audit evidence. All entries include owner, date, and approval status.

📅 Review & actions

Check the Document Review Register for upcoming review dates and pending approvals across all GRC documents.

GRC principles

📋 Documented & approved

All GRC decisions are documented, owned, and formally approved before being treated as authoritative

⏱️ Time-bound

All exceptions, risk acceptances, and supplier assessments have defined review dates — nothing is open-ended

👤 Owned

Every register entry, policy, and exception has a named owner accountable for its currency and accuracy

🔍 Auditable

All GRC activities generate evidence suitable for internal and external audit review

🔄 Continuously reviewed

GRC documents are living — reviewed on schedule and updated following incidents, changes, or audit findings
