GRC-FRM-13: Risk Assessment & Submission Form

Used to identify, analyse, and submit a new cyber or IT risk for inclusion in the Cyber & IT Risk Register. Aligned with GRC-SOP-04, ISO 31000, ISO 27001:2022, and NIST CSF 2.0.

📌 Completion instructions: Complete every section. The submitter scores inherent and residual risk; the GRC team validates scoring before adding to the register.

For likelihood and impact scoring criteria, refer to GRC-SOP-04 §4a / §4b. Inline summaries are provided below.

Submit completed forms to the GRC team. Once accepted into the register, the risk receives a unique ID (RISK-NNN).

Declaration: The submitter confirms that the information provided is accurate to the best of their knowledge, that the risk has been described without exaggeration or omission, and that the proposed treatment is realistic and proportionate.
