Outcome Thresholds

85–100 → Approve

Strong controls — proceed to onboarding with standard conditions where applicable

70–84 → Approve with Conditions

Adequate controls with identified gaps — document conditions and reassessment requirements

55–69 → Escalate for Risk Treatment

Material weaknesses identified — escalate for further assessment or formal risk acceptance

Below 55 → Not Approved

Significant control failures — do not approve unless exceptional risk acceptance applies

Mandatory Escalation Criteria — escalate irrespective of score if any apply

No MFA for remote or privileged support access
Shared or unattributable support administration
No logging of engineer activity in customer environments
Uncontrolled access to live data during troubleshooting
No formal change control for patches, fixes, or upgrades
No clear subcontractor oversight where third parties may access the environment
No incident notification route for support-related compromise
