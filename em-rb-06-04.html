EM-RB-06-04: Escalation, Communication & Closure

Ensure safety, compliance, and controlled closure of the threatening email incident — covering stakeholder notification, user communication, evidence preservation, and formal closure.

Section 1
Objective

Ensure user safety, meet compliance obligations, preserve evidence with proper chain of custody, and close or transition the incident in a controlled and documented manner.

Section 2
Trigger & Invocation
🔗 Tier-3 escalation from EM-PB-06 🔗 Or: containment completed in EM-RB-06-03 👥 Executed by SOC Tier-3
Section 3
Main Run — Escalation & Closure Procedure
1
Escalation & Stakeholder Notification

Notify the following stakeholders based on severity and incident type:

⚖️
Legal / Compliance
If regulatory obligations may apply
👥
HR
For employee support and welfare
🏢
Leadership / Crisis Team
If P1 severity — critical or widespread threat
2
User Communication
1
Inform all affected users that the email has been removed and the situation is being managed
Use an approved, out-of-band communication channel — do not reply via the compromised thread
2
Provide clear instructions on what users should do if they receive further messages
3
Reassure users and set clear expectations — confirm that their safety and well-being is the priority
3
Evidence Preservation
1
Secure all email artefacts collected throughout the series (headers, body, attachments, URLs)
2
Retain all logs and documented actions — do not delete or modify evidence
3
Maintain chain of custody — record who handled evidence and when
This is critical if the incident proceeds to law enforcement or legal action
4
Closure
1
Document all actions taken throughout the four-runbook series in the incident ticket
2
Record lessons learned and any detection or process improvement recommendations
3
Close or transition the incident per the governance process defined in EM-PB-06
Section 4
Closure Checklist

Confirm all of the following before closing the incident:

All stakeholders notified as required by severity
All affected users communicated with
All evidence preserved with chain of custody documented
All actions documented in the incident ticket
Lessons learned recorded
Incident closed or formally transitioned
Section 5
Outputs

✅ Incident formally closed or transitioned
✅ Evidence preserved and chain of custody maintained
✅ Improvement actions logged for governance review

Closed incident record
Preserved evidence package
Lessons learned entry
Stakeholder communication record
