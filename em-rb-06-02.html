EM-RB-06-02: Threat & Credibility Analysis

Section 1
Objective

Determine the intent, credibility, and severity of the threatening or harassing email to inform escalation and containment decisions.

Section 2
Trigger & Invocation
🔗 Tier-1 escalation from EM-RB-06-01 👥 Executed by SOC Tier-2
Section 3
Main Run — Analysis Procedure
1
Content Analysis
1
Analyse the email content for the following indicators:
  • Explicit threats — specific harm, violence, or legal action threatened
  • Implicit intimidation — language designed to cause fear without explicit threat
  • Demands or timelines — deadlines, ultimatums, or financial requests
  • Personal or internal references — names, locations, access to internal information
2
Sender Analysis
1
Determine whether the sender is external or spoofing an internal address
2
Check the sending domain age and reputation
Newly registered or low-reputation domains increase credibility of malicious intent
3
Review authentication results — SPF, DKIM, and DMARC pass/fail status
3
Infrastructure Analysis
1
Check URL reputation and follow redirects to identify final destinations
2
Review attachment metadata and calculate file hashes
3
Check for tracking pixels, beaconing links, or read-receipt indicators
Tracking indicators suggest the attacker may be monitoring whether the email was opened
4
Campaign Assessment
1
Search for previous sightings of the same sender, domain, or message content
2
Identify similar language patterns or shared infrastructure across incidents
3
Assess whether indicators suggest coordinated or campaign-level activity
Section 4
Decision Points

After completing analysis, apply the following decision logic:

✅ No credible threat identified

Proceed to containment — invoke EM-RB-06-03 to remove emails and block sender

🔴 Credible threat identified

Tier-3 escalation mandatory — invoke EM-PB-06 escalation path before proceeding to containment

Section 5
Outputs

✅ Updated severity classification based on analysis
✅ Clear escalation recommendation documented
✅ Enriched incident record with sender, infrastructure, and campaign findings
