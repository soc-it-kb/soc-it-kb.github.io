GRC · Compliance

✅ Compliance

Baseline compliance tracking, control library, validation runs, and drift remediation registers that evidence the organisation's ongoing compliance posture against approved security baselines and frameworks.

Baseline compliance Control library Validation runs Drift management Audit evidence
Overview
What this domain covers

📦 Baseline Catalog

The authoritative list of approved security baselines in scope — endpoint, server, DC, PAW, and Linux — with version, owner, and status.

📚 Control Library

The library of individual controls that make up each baseline — mapped to CIS Benchmarks, NIST CSF 2.0, and asset types.

✅ Validation Runs

Records of scheduled and triggered baseline validation activities — what was checked, when, by whom, and the outcome.

🔄 Drift & Remediation

Tracks detected baseline drift events, classification (approved / operational / unapproved), remediation actions, and closure status.
